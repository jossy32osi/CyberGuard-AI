# CyberGuard AI — Vision Document

## Project Overview

CyberGuard AI is an AI-powered cybersecurity assistant designed to help users identify phishing attempts, scam messages, fraudulent websites, and other online threats before they become victims.

## Mission Statement

To reduce digital fraud and improve cyber awareness by providing fast, accurate, and easy-to-understand security guidance for everyday internet users.

## Vision Statement

To become a trusted AI-driven cyber safety platform that empowers millions of people to use digital services confidently and securely.

## Problem Statement

Nigerians are increasingly targeted by phishing attacks, fake investment schemes, fraudulent job offers, impersonation scams, and malicious websites. Many people lack the knowledge or tools to quickly determine whether a message, website, or email is genuine, leading to financial loss and identity theft.

## Objectives

* Detect phishing attempts and scam messages.
* Analyze suspicious URLs and websites.
* Provide clear explanations of detected risks.
* Educate users on cybersecurity best practices.
* Improve confidence in using digital services safely.

## Target Users

### Primary Users

* Students
* Small business owners
* Traders
* Parents
* Job seekers
* Civil servants

### Secondary Users

* Schools
* Banks
* Cybersecurity awareness organizations

## Expected Impact

CyberGuard AI aims to reduce scam victimization, improve cybersecurity awareness, and help users make safer digital decisions before clicking links, sharing information, or sending money.

## Scope (Version 1)

The initial version will support:

* Text message analysis
* Email analysis
* URL analysis
* Screenshot upload and analysis
* Risk scoring
* Cyber Safety Reports
* Security recommendations

## Success Metrics

The project will be considered successful if users can:

* Understand why a message is suspicious.
* Identify high-risk scams quickly.
* Make safer online decisions.
* Improve their cybersecurity awareness over time.
